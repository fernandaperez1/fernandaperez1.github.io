My favorite characters

Muffin (マフィン Mafin) is a smart little brown-and-white hamster and Pompompurin's best friend and playmate. He will eat just about anything and loves to crack open nuts and store them in his cheeks. Muffin also enjoys hiding inside Pompompurin's beret. He lives next door to Pompompurin. He's shrewd and loves eating, but he's really messy when he does.

Scone (スコーン Sukōn) is a cute little mouse who lives in a red gumboot in a field. He's a sensible, thoughtful, and hardworking yellow mouse with a brown lining in his ears. He can do tricks with his tail, like picking up chestnuts. Scone enjoys corn-eating contests. He met his friend Pompompurin through a hamster named Muffin.

Pompompurin (ポムポムプリン, Pomupomupurin), sometimes shortend as Purin or Pompom, is a character introduced by the Japanese company Sanrio and is the main protagonist of the Pompompurin universe. A yellow Golden Retriever, Purin was born on a sunny day on April 16th. Pompompurin lives in his own basket in the entrance hall of his sister-owner's house. His trademark is his brown beret that is always on top of his head. Pompompurin's favorite food is his mama's pudding.
